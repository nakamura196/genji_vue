The Digital Tale of Genji has been nominated for the Digital Humanities Awards 2025 (DH Award 2025)!

For all the projects entered in DH Award 2025 and information about DH Award 2025, please see the following site.

<http://dhawards.org/>

Among the entries, the Winners are decided by your votes. We would be grateful if you could vote for the Digital Tale of Genji using the form at the bottom of the above page. *The Digital Tale of Genji is in the category "DH RESOURCE (e.g. digital scholarly edition, archive, or repository for DH)".

We look forward to your votes!

\-----------------

May 9, 2026 — Update

The [results of DH Award 2025](http://dhawards.org/dhawards2025/results/) have been announced.
Unfortunately, the Digital Tale of Genji did not receive an award.

However, we are deeply grateful that the project was entered into DH Awards 2025, and we would like to take this opportunity to thank everyone who voted for us.

<http://dhawards.org/dhawards2025/results/>
