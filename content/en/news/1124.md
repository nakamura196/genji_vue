---
title: Publication of the Chinese translation of "Utilizing Digital Data for Building a Research Platform"
date: 2025-05-23
---

<p>A Chinese translation has been published of the paper "<a href="https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/jcul/126/0/126_2165/_article/-char/ja">Utilizing Digital Data for Building a Research Platform: An Attempt to Publish the "Digital Tale of Genji" through Faculty-Staff Collaboration</a>," which was originally published in June 2024 in the journal University Library Studies. The translation was made possible by an offer from Mr. Wang Zirui of Beijing Language and Culture University, who read the paper and expressed a desire to translate it into Chinese.</p><p>The translation is freely available to anyone on the Digital Humanities Research website.<br><a href="http://dhr.ruc.edu.cn/CN/Y2025/V5/I1/54">http://dhr.ruc.edu.cn/CN/Y2025/V5/I1/54</a></p><p>We would like to take this opportunity to express our gratitude to Mr. Wang Zirui of Beijing Language and Culture University for carrying out the Chinese translation and making its publication possible.</p>
