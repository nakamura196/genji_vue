title: In partnership with Digital Tale of Genji - Publication of "The Tale of Genji in Textbooks LOD"
date: 2025-08-06
---

"<a href="https://www2.u-gakugei.ac.jp/~library/genjitext/index.html">The Tale of Genji in Textbooks LOD</a>" by the Tokyo Gakugei University Library has been published.<br>This site publishes data on The Tale of Genji as it appears in postwar government-approved high school classical literature textbooks as Linked Open Data (LOD).<br><a href="https://www2.u-gakugei.ac.jp/~library/genjitext/index.html">https://www2.u-gakugei.ac.jp/~library/genjitext/index.html</a>

The detailed information for each chapter includes links to our Digital Tale of Genji's "<a href="https://genji.dl.itc.u-tokyo.ac.jp/main">View Images and Text Together</a>" page.<br>Please make use of "The Tale of Genji in Textbooks LOD" as well.
