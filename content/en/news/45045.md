---
title: Search functionality improved in the "AI Image Search"
date: 2025-06-25
---

The text search of the Koi Genji Monogatari (Collated Tale of Genji) in the <a href="https://genji-ai.web.app/">"AI Image Search"</a> has been improved so that you can now search without worrying about differences in historical kana usage or voiced/unvoiced consonants. A <a href="https://genji-ai.web.app/search-help">search help page</a> has also been published.

Please continue to make use of the "AI Image Search".
