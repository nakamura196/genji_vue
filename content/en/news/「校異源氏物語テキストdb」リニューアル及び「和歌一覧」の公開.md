---
title: Renewal of the "Kōi Genji Monogatari Text DB" and publication of the "Waka List"
date: 2026-03-15
---

The following three corrections and publications have been made regarding the text data of the Kōi Genji Monogatari (Collated Tale of Genji).

1. Typographical errors in the Kōi Genji Monogatari text data have been corrected based on feedback from users.
2. The "Kōi Genji Monogatari Text DB" has been renewed. In addition to the previously available formats, the data can now be downloaded in PDF and other formats. Please make use of it.

   <https://kouigenjimonogatari.github.io/>
3. A "Waka List" page has been published as part of the "Kōi Genji Monogatari Text DB." You can view a list of all 795 poems from the 54 chapters of The Tale of Genji. You can also view a graph of the number of waka appearing in each chapter.

   <https://kouigenjimonogatari.github.io/waka.html>
